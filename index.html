Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Clear, direct answers regarding custom fabric pricing frameworks and structural setup timelines.

❓ What is the estimated price for custom curtains?
Pricing depends completely on window dimensions and selected fabric quality. We offer direct-from-factory rates without any retailer middleman markups. Contact our specialists with rough sizing values for a precise preliminary estimate.
❓ How fast is the production and complete installation timeline?
Once your fabric choice and custom design configurations are locked down, fabric production, cutting, sewing, and final structural rail setup take between 1 to 3 working days.
❓ Is there a minimum window order required to secure a booking?
No, there is no strict minimum project size restriction. We confidently cater to small single-bedroom updates, full residential terraced houses, or commercial office floor renovations.
❓ Can your local team manage curtain installations in high-rise condos?
Yes, our logistics and technical drilling installation teams are fully experienced with Kuala Lumpur and Selangor condo management management protocols, working hours, and security clearance requirements.
❓ Are your window on-site measurement consultations completely free?
Yes. Our specialists visit your property location across KL and Selangor, execute proper laser window sizing, present physical fabric catalogs, and detail quotes with zero underlying obligations.
❓ What premium fabric options do you carry?
We supply an expansive inventory, including 100% thick thermal blackouts, linen-textures, modern day sheer materials, anti-UV screens, and fire-resistant commercial drapery options.
